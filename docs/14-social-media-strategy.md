**Goal:** Build Dr. Adnan Jabbar's authority and trust across Pakistan's dominant social platforms, converting followers into WhatsApp consultation inquiries

## Executive Summary

In Pakistan, patients discover doctors on Google but make trust decisions on Instagram and Facebook. For a fertility specialist, social media is not about going viral — it is about building the slow, deep trust required before someone shares their most private health struggle with a doctor they have never met.

Dr. Adnan Jabbar has a compelling differentiator that almost no Pakistani fertility specialist has communicated publicly: dual training as both clinician and embryologist. This is the social media story. Every post, video, and story should reinforce one message — this doctor understands your case from both sides of the laboratory glass.

Instagram is the primary platform for Pakistani urban healthcare audiences (25–44). Facebook reaches the broader and older demographic and enables paid lead generation. YouTube builds long-term SEO authority through patient education videos. The key to making this sustainable is a one-session-to-many-pieces content system: 45 minutes of Dr. Adnan filming each week produces content across all platforms for the entire month.

**North Star Metric:** WhatsApp consultation inquiries originating from social media.
