# Content Strategy — ivfexperts.pk
**Assessed:** 2026-03-03 | **Status:** 🔴 Not Started
**Market:** Pakistani couples (English-reading), diaspora, international patients
**Primary goal:** Organic traffic → consultation bookings via WhatsApp/form

---

## Executive Summary

IVF Experts has 35+ excellent treatment/condition pages but almost no blog content strategy. The massive opportunity: Pakistan's fertility content landscape is nearly empty in English. There is little well-researched, medically accurate, patient-friendly English content about fertility treatment *in the Pakistani context* — costs in PKR, available hospitals, cultural considerations, local treatment options. This is a blue ocean. Owning 5 content pillars with 6–8 blog posts each = estimated 20,000–50,000 additional monthly organic visits within 12 months.

**Content format priority:** Written blog posts → Guides → Comparison pages → FAQ pages → Free tools (linked from content)

---

## PILLAR 1: IVF & ART Procedures

**Hub:** `/art-procedures/` (upgrade to 800+ word pillar page)

### ✅ Task 1: Write "IVF Success Rates in Pakistan — What to Realistically Expect" (Data Page)
- **Type:** Data + authority (uniquely citable)
- **Target keyword:** "IVF success rate Pakistan" (~500 searches/mo)
- **Why:** Patients desperately want local data. If Dr. Adnan publishes his own clinic's outcomes (even anonymized ranges), this becomes the most-cited page on the site.
- **Content:** Success rates by age bracket, by diagnosis, by protocol. Compare to HFEA/SART international benchmarks. Include: "Why success rates vary and what you can do to improve yours."
- **CTA:** "Get a personalized assessment of your success chances"
- **AI SEO value:** Very high — original data is cited 3–5x more often.
- **Length:** 1500–2000 words + tables
- **Priority:** 🔴 High

### ✅ Task 2: Write "IVF vs ICSI — Which Treatment Is Right for You?"
- **Type:** Comparison (high AI citation potential)
- **Target keyword:** "IVF vs ICSI" / "difference between IVF and ICSI Pakistan"
- **Format:** Comparison table + 5 key differences + "When is ICSI recommended?" + FAQ
- **Length:** 1200 words
- **Priority:** 🔴 High

### ✅ Task 3: Write "IVF Cost in Pakistan 2026 — Complete Breakdown"
- **Type:** Pricing transparency (high intent, almost zero competition)
- **Target keyword:** "IVF cost in Pakistan 2026" / "IVF price Lahore"
- **Content:** Break down all cost components in PKR: consultation, medications (stimulation drugs are the biggest variable), egg retrieval, embryology lab fees, embryo transfer, genetic testing (optional), monitoring scans. Explain why costs vary. Add: "Red flags when a clinic quotes too low."
- **Link to:** IVF Cost Estimator tool (once built)
- **Length:** 1500 words
- **Priority:** 🔴 High

### ✅ Task 4: Write "What to Expect During Your IVF Cycle — Week by Week"
- **Type:** How-to / patient education
- **Target keyword:** "IVF process step by step Pakistan" / "what happens during IVF"
- **Format:** Day-by-day or week-by-week timeline. Reduce patient anxiety by making the process predictable.
- **CTA:** IVF Timeline Calculator (when built)
- **Length:** 1200 words
- **Priority:** 🟡 Medium

### ✅ Task 5: Write "PGT-A Genetic Testing — Is It Right for You?"
- **Type:** Decision guide
- **Target keyword:** "PGT testing IVF Pakistan" / "genetic testing embryo Pakistan"
- **Content:** What PGT-A tests for, when it's recommended (recurrent miscarriage, advanced maternal age, known genetic conditions), cost, limitations, ethical considerations.
- **Length:** 1000 words
- **Priority:** 🟡 Medium

### ✅ Task 6: Write "Gender Selection in Pakistan — Medical and Legal Facts"
- **Type:** Informational + sensitive topic navigation
- **Target keyword:** "gender selection IVF Pakistan" / "PGT gender selection Pakistan"
- **Why:** High search volume, significant cultural interest, lots of misinformation. A clear, medically accurate, culturally sensitive article fills a real gap.
- **Content:** How PGT-based family balancing works, legal status in Pakistan, medical process, ethical framework, FAQs.
- **Length:** 1200 words
- **Priority:** 🟡 Medium

## Content Standards

Every blog post must:
- [ ] Have a clear primary keyword in title, H1, first paragraph, and URL
- [ ] Open with a 40–60 word definition or context paragraph (AI extractable)
- [ ] Include 3+ cited statistics (source + year)
- [ ] Use H2 headings that match question formats ("What is X?", "How does X work?")
- [ ] Include a FAQ section (5+ Q&A pairs) with FAQPage schema
- [ ] Have a visible "Last Updated: [Month Year]" date
- [ ] Include Dr. Adnan's name and credentials (E-E-A-T)
- [ ] End with a WhatsApp CTA
- [ ] Be 800+ words minimum (1200–2000 words for pillar posts)
- [ ] Link to 2–3 related internal pages
- [ ] Link to 1–2 relevant external authoritative sources (WHO, HFEA, etc.)
